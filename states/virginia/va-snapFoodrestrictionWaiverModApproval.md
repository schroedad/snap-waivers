Dear Principal Deputy Commissioner Ayers:

On February 3, 2026, the Virginia Department of Social Services requested to modify the State
Agency’s Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) Food Restriction Waiver
implementation date from April 1, 2026, to October 1, 2026.

Per Virginia’s request letter, this modification is intended to provide necessary additional time
for the State Agency to align implementation activities with updated administrative priorities and
ensure all operational, systems, and stakeholder readiness components are in place.

The Food and Nutrition Service (FNS) approves this modification request. This approval is for 2
years, from October 1, 2026, through September 30, 2028. All other terms and conditions of
FNS's December 10, 2025, approval remain in effect.

FNS applauds Virginia and its SNAP team for the significant efforts you have made towards
implementation of your SNAP Food Restriction pilot thus far. We look forward to continuing to
support your efforts to successfully implement this innovative and impactful project on October
1, 2026.

Acting Administrator, Food and Nutrition Service
