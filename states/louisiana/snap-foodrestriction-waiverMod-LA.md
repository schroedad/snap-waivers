Re: SNAP Food Restriction Demonstration Waiver Modification Request

Dear Secretary Greenstein:

On December 4, 2025, the Louisiana Department of Health (LDH) requested to modify the State
agency’s Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) Food Restriction Waiver
implementation date from January 1, 2026, to February 18, 2026.

The Food and Nutrition Service (FNS) approves this modification request. As expressed in the
State agency’s request letter, this modification is intended to provide necessary additional time
to ensure accurate application of policy, prevent erroneous transactions, and support consistent
communication to SNAP households and authorized retailers. FNS agrees that this
modification will provide a stronger foundation for project success.

This approval is for 2 years, effective February 18, 2026, through February 17, 2028. FNS
provides the modified restricted food definitions and the adjusted project reporting schedule in
the enclosure. All other information, terms and conditions of FNS’ August 4, 2025, waiver
approval remain in effect.

FNS applauds Louisiana and its SNAP team for the significant efforts made towards the
implementation of the state’s SNAP Food Restriction Waiver thus far. We look forward to
continuing to support your efforts to successfully implement this innovative and impactful
project on February 18, 2026.

Deputy Under Secretary
