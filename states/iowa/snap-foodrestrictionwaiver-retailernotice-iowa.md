**Beginning January 1, 2026, Iowa SNAP retailers cannot accept SNAP EBT payments for taxable food items,**
**(including** **candy, prepared foods, soft drinks, and other sweetened drinks), except seeds and plants that grow food,**
**as defined by the Iowa Department of Revenue.**

You will need to comply with these changes. **If you do not comply, FNS may withdraw your SNAP**
**authorization, and you will no longer be able to accept SNAP EBT.**

For more information on Iowa Department of Revenue taxable versus non-taxable items, please visit the Iowa
Department of Health & Human Services’ [SNAP Retailer webpage and](https://hhs.iowa.gov/assistance-programs/food-assistance/snap/snap-retailers) [Iowa Sales Tax on Food webpage.](https://revenue.iowa.gov/taxes/tax-guidance/sales-use-excise-tax/iowa-sales-tax-food)

If you have questions, please contact [SNAPRetailer@hhs.Iowa.gov.](mailto:SNAPRetailer@hhs.Iowa.gov)

For a complete list of states implementing similar changes and to review the [Iowa SNAP Food Restricton](https://www.fns.usda.gov/snap/waivers/foodrestriction/iowa)
[Waiver approval, visit USDA's](https://www.fns.usda.gov/snap/waivers/foodrestriction/iowa) [SNAP Food Restricton Waivers.](https://www.fns.usda.gov/snap/waivers/foodrestriction)
