Re: SNAP Food Restriction Demonstration Waiver Modification Request

Dear Director Adams:

On September 4, 2025, the Idaho Department of Health and Welfare requested to modify the
State agency’s Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) Food Restriction Waiver
implementation date from January 1, 2026, to February 15, 2026.

The Food and Nutrition Service (FNS) approves this modification request. As expressed in the
State agency’s request letter, this modification is intended to provide necessary additional time
for SNAP-authorized retailers to prepare for implementation. Postponing implementation to
February 15, 2026, will prevent inconsistent implementation amongst retailers and provide a
stronger foundation for success.

This approval is for 2 years, from February 15, 2026, through February 15, 2028. FNS provides
an adjusted project reporting schedule in the enclosure. All other terms and conditions of FNS's
June 10, 2025, approval remain applicable.

FNS applauds Idaho and its SNAP team for the significant efforts you have made towards
implementation of your SNAP Food Restriction Waiver thus far. We look forward to continuing
to support your efforts to successfully implement this innovative and impactful project on
February 15, 2026.

Deputy Under Secretary
